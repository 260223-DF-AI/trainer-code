# Project 3: The Multi-Modal Intelligent Pipeline

## Choose Your Track

### Overview

Your team has been tasked with building an intelligent, cloud-integrated pipeline that combines **Computer Vision** (local training) and **LLM Reasoning** (cloud prompts). You must choose **ONE** of the following tracks to implement. Each track requires the exact same technical stack but solves a different industry problem.

---

### Project Tracks

#### Track A: SecureContent AI (Moderation & Response)

- **The Problem**: A social media platform needs to automate moderation and community outreach.
- **Vision Task**: Classify user images as `Professional`, `Social`, or `Flagged (Unsafe)`.
- **Reasoning Task**: Use **ReAct** prompting to analyze the user's comment and decide if the post is approved or if an automated "Policy Violation" response should be generated.

#### Track B: SmartLogistics (Damage & Claim Assistant)

- **The Problem**: A logistics firm needs to automate the "Damaged Goods" claim process.
- **Vision Task**: Classify cargo/parcel photos as `Intact`, `Damaged`, or `Incorrect Labeling`.
- **Reasoning Task**: Use **Chain of Thought (CoT)** to assess the claim severity from a text description and use **ReAct** to decide if a refund is issued or a technician is dispatched.

#### Track C: EcoTrack (Wildlife Monitor & Conservation)

- **The Problem**: A conservation group needs to monitor endangered species in trail camera footage.
- **Vision Task**: Identify animal species (e.g., `Deer`, `Bear`, `Endangered Species X`).
- **Reasoning Task**: Use **Graph of Thought (GoT)** to analyze location/weather logs and **ReAct** to draft a conservation alert if an endangered species is identified in an unusual location.

#### Track D: EstateInsight (Property Valuer & Agent)

- **The Problem**: A real-estate firm needs to scale their property quality assessments.
- **Vision Task**: Classify room types and assessment quality (e.g., `Kitchen-Renovated`, `Bathroom-Original`, `Exterior-Basic`).
- **Reasoning Task**: Use **Tree of Thought (ToT)** to explore pricing strategies and **ReAct** to generate a "Sales Pitch" based on the photo quality and neighborhood description.

#### Track E: AgriTech (Crop Health & Treatment)

- **The Problem**: An autonomous farming collective needs to monitor crop health and automate treatment advice.
- **Vision Task**: Classify crop leaf images as `Healthy`, `Pest-Infested`, or `Nutrient-Deficient`.
- **Reasoning Task**: Use **Chain of Thought (CoT)** to analyze soil/weather logs and **ReAct** to recommend a specific treatment plan or irrigation adjustment.

#### Track F: RetailGuard (Inventory & Safety Monitor)

- **The Problem**: A smart-retailer needs to manage shop floor safety and shelf stock automatically.
- **Vision Task**: Identify safety hazards (e.g., `Liquid-Spill`, `Obstruction`) or shelf status (`Empty`, `Stocked`).
- **Reasoning Task**: Use **Tree of Thought (ToT)** to evaluate the priority of restocking vs. hazard cleanup and **ReAct** to alert the correct staff member or update inventory orders.

#### Track G: AutoIntel (Driver Safety & Diagnostics)

- **The Problem**: A fleet management company needs to monitor driver alertness and vehicle dashboard health.
- **Vision Task**: Classify driver status (`Alert`, `Drowsy`, `Distracted`) or dashboard warning lights.
- **Reasoning Task**: Use **Dialog State** management to handle driver alerts and **ReAct** to suggest the nearest rest stop or service center based on GPS and vehicle status.

---

### Universal Technical Requirements

Regardless of your track, your submission must meet the following criteria:

#### 1. Computer Vision (Moderation Engine)

- **Model Architecture**: Use a custom `nn.Module` or a fine-tuned ResNet backbone.
- **Optimization**: Implement **Mixed Precision (AMP)** and **Early Stopping** based on a validation set.
- **Deployment**: Launch the training job via **SageMaker Script Mode**. Artifacts must be registered in the **SageMaker Model Registry**.

#### 2. Advanced Prompting (Reasoning & Response)

- **Cognitive Blueprint**: You must implement at least two Prompting Paradigms (e.g., **CoT**, **ToT**, **GoT**, or **Dialog State**).
- **Orchestration**: Implement a **ReAct** (Reasoning + Acting) loop to finalize the decision based on vision + text inputs.
- **Safety**: Implement an **Input Sanitization** layer (Week 3 TUE) to prevent malicious prompt injection.

#### 3. Integrated Service (System API)

- **FastAPI**: A `POST /analyze` endpoint that accepts a file and text input.
- **Persistence**: Store all audit logs in a **PostgreSQL** database, including CNN confidence scores, LLM reasoning, and the final generated output.
